Tips for Finding More Secrets

If you want to discover more hidden content, try exploring without a specific goal. Drive through remote countryside roads, climb mountains, and explore underwater areas. Using different vehicles or visiting locations at unusual times can reveal details you may have missed before.

Explore at Different Times: Lighting changes, NPC behavior, and environmental effects sometimes make certain secrets easier to notice.

Check Remote Areas: Countryside roads, mountains, and underwater locations often hide the best secrets.
